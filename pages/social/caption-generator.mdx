## What is the Caption Generator?

The **Caption Generator** (located at **Social > Caption Generator**) is an AI-powered tool that helps you write compelling social media captions quickly.

Instead of staring at a blank page, you provide a brief description of the content you want to post, and the AI generates several caption options for you to choose from, edit, and publish.

## How to Use the Caption Generator

1. Navigate to **Social > Caption Generator**.
2. In the **Topic or Description** field, describe what your post is about. Be as specific as possible for better results.
   - *Example*: "A photo of our team at the annual company retreat in Banff, Alberta. We want to highlight our company culture and team spirit."
3. Select the **Tone** you want the caption to have (e.g., Professional, Casual, Humorous, Inspirational).
4. Select the **Platform** you are writing for (e.g., LinkedIn, Instagram, X/Twitter). The AI will adjust the length and style accordingly.
5. Optionally, provide **Keywords or Hashtags** you want the AI to include.
6. Click **Generate Captions**.

The AI will produce 3-5 different caption variations for you to review.

## Reviewing and Editing Captions

After the AI generates its suggestions, you can:

- **Select a Caption**: Click on the caption you like best.
- **Edit**: Modify the selected caption to add your personal touch or correct any details.
- **Regenerate**: If none of the suggestions are quite right, click **Regenerate** to get a new set of options. You can also adjust your description or tone to guide the AI in a different direction.

## Sending to the Post Composer

Once you are happy with a caption:

1. Click **Use This Caption**.
2. The caption will be automatically inserted into the **Post Composer** in **Social > Posts**.
3. Select your target social profiles, attach any media, and publish or schedule the post.

## Tips for Better Results

The quality of the AI's output is directly related to the quality of your input. Here are some tips for getting the best captions:

- **Be specific about the content**: Instead of "a product photo," say "a photo of our new blue wireless headphones on a white background."
- **Specify your goal**: Tell the AI what you want the post to achieve (e.g., "drive clicks to our website," "increase engagement," "announce a new feature").
- **Provide brand context**: If you have a very specific brand voice, include a brief description (e.g., "Our brand is witty and irreverent, like Wendy's Twitter account").
- **Iterate**: Don't settle for the first generation. Refine your prompt and regenerate until you get something you love.
