## What are Social Profiles?

In Autoch.at, a **Social Profile** represents a single social media account belonging to your brand (e.g., your company's Facebook Page, Instagram business account, or X/Twitter profile).

The **Social Profiles** page (located at **Social > Social Profiles**) is the master list of all your connected social identities. From here, you can see the status of each connection, add new profiles, and manage existing ones.

## Why Connect Social Profiles?

Connecting your social profiles to Autoch.at allows you to:

1. **Unify Your Inbox**: Direct messages sent to your social accounts will appear in the [Conversations Inbox](/pages/conversations/conversations) alongside your chat, SMS, and email threads.
2. **AI-Powered Responses**: Your AI assistants can be configured to automatically respond to direct messages on social media, just as they do on other channels.
3. **Publish Content**: Schedule and publish posts to your connected profiles directly from Autoch.at, without needing to log in to each platform separately.
4. **Monitor Performance**: View engagement metrics (likes, comments, shares, reach) for your published content in [Social Analytics](/pages/social/social-analytics).

## Connecting a Social Profile

To connect a new social media account:

1. Navigate to **Social > Social Profiles**.
2. Click **Connect Profile** or **Add Account**.
3. Select the social media platform you want to connect (e.g., Facebook, Instagram, X/Twitter).
4. You will be redirected to the platform's authorization page.
5. Log in to the social media account you want to connect and grant Autoch.at the necessary permissions.
6. You will be returned to Autoch.at, and the new profile will appear in your list.

<Callout kind="info">
**Business Accounts Only**: Autoch.at connects to business and professional accounts, not personal profiles. Ensure you are logged in to the correct account type before authorizing the connection.
</Callout>

## Managing Connected Profiles

From the Social Profiles page, you can manage each connected account.

- **View Status**: See if the connection is active and healthy, or if there is an issue (e.g., the authorization token has expired and needs to be refreshed).
- **Reconnect**: If a connection breaks (this can happen when a social platform updates its API or when you change your password), click **Reconnect** to re-authorize the integration.
- **Disconnect**: Remove a social profile from Autoch.at. This will stop syncing messages and prevent new posts from being published to that account.

## Assigning Profiles to Workspaces

If your Autoch.at organization has multiple [Workspaces](/pages/settings/workspaces) (e.g., one for each brand or department), you can assign specific social profiles to specific workspaces.

This ensures that the marketing team for Brand A only sees and manages Brand A's social accounts, while Brand B's team manages their own.
