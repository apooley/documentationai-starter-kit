## What is Social Analytics?

**Social Analytics** (located at **Social > Analytics**) provides a reporting view for the content you publish through Autoch.at's social media tools.

It aggregates engagement data from your connected social profiles, allowing you to understand which types of content resonate with your audience and optimize your publishing strategy accordingly.

## Key Metrics

Social Analytics tracks the following engagement metrics for your published posts:

| Metric | Description |
|---|---|
| **Reach** | The total number of unique accounts that saw your post. |
| **Impressions** | The total number of times your post was displayed (a single account can generate multiple impressions). |
| **Likes / Reactions** | The number of likes, hearts, or other reactions your post received. |
| **Comments** | The number of comments left on your post. |
| **Shares / Reposts** | The number of times your post was shared or reposted by other accounts. |
| **Clicks** | The number of times a link in your post was clicked. |
| **Engagement Rate** | A calculated percentage: (Total Engagements / Reach) × 100. A higher rate indicates more compelling content. |

## Filtering and Comparing

At the top of the Social Analytics page, you can filter the data to focus on what matters most to you.

- **Time Range**: Select a specific period to analyze (e.g., Last 7 Days, Last 30 Days, Last Quarter).
- **Social Profile**: View metrics for all profiles combined, or drill down into a single profile (e.g., only Facebook, or only Instagram).
- **Content Type**: Filter by post type (e.g., only image posts, only video posts, only text posts).

## Top Performing Posts

The **Top Posts** section highlights the content that generated the most engagement during your selected time range. Reviewing your top performers helps you identify patterns:

- What topics generate the most interest?
- Do image posts outperform text posts?
- Which days or times drive the highest engagement?

Use these insights to inform your content strategy and the types of posts you add to your [Post Queues](/pages/social/social-queues).

## Audience Growth

If your connected social platforms provide audience data, Social Analytics will also display your follower growth over time. This helps you understand the long-term impact of your publishing activity on your brand's reach.

<Callout kind="info">
**Platform Limitations**: The availability of specific metrics depends on what each social media platform's API provides. Some platforms may not expose all metrics to third-party tools like Autoch.at.
</Callout>
