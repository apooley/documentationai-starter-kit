## Measure your social performance

The **Social Analytics** page (at **Social Media > Analytics**) gives you a consolidated view of how your social media accounts are performing. Instead of checking each platform's native analytics separately, you can see all your key metrics in one place.

## What's tracked

### Engagement metrics

| Metric | What it measures |
|---|---|
| **Likes / Reactions** | How many people reacted positively to your posts |
| **Comments** | How many comments your posts received |
| **Shares / Reposts** | How many times your content was shared |
| **Clicks** | How many people clicked links in your posts |
| **Engagement rate** | Total engagements as a percentage of reach |

### Reach and impressions

- **Reach** — The number of unique accounts that saw your content.
- **Impressions** — The total number of times your content was displayed (one person can generate multiple impressions).

### Follower growth

Track how your follower count changes over time for each connected account.

### DM response metrics

Since Autoch.at handles your social DMs, you can also see:

- **DMs received** — Total incoming direct messages per platform
- **AI-handled** — DMs resolved by your AI assistant without human intervention
- **Average response time** — How quickly DMs received their first response

## Filtering the data

Use the filters at the top of the page to:

- **Select accounts** — View data for all accounts or a specific one
- **Select date range** — Day, week, month, or custom range
- **Select metric type** — Focus on engagement, reach, or DM metrics

## Exporting data

Click **Export** to download your social analytics data as a CSV file for use in your own reports or dashboards.

<Callout kind="info">
Social analytics data is pulled from each platform's API and may be delayed by up to a few hours. Real-time data is not available.
</Callout>
