## What are CRM Connections?

While Autoch.at has a built-in [Clients](/pages/crm/clients) database, many businesses already use a dedicated Customer Relationship Management (CRM) system like Salesforce, HubSpot, or Pipedrive as their central source of truth.

**CRM Connections** (located at **CRM > CRM Connections**) allow you to link Autoch.at to your existing CRM. This ensures that data flows seamlessly between the two platforms, keeping your records accurate and your team informed.

## Why Connect Your CRM?

Connecting your CRM provides several key benefits:

1. **Unified Data**: When a new lead interacts with an Autoch.at assistant, their contact information is automatically pushed to your CRM.
2. **Contextual Conversations**: Autoch.at can pull data from your CRM (e.g., account status, recent purchases) to provide the AI assistant with more context, enabling highly personalized responses.
3. **Activity Logging**: Every conversation a customer has with an AI assistant or human agent in Autoch.at is logged as an activity on their CRM record.
4. **Workflow Automation**: You can trigger Autoch.at workflows based on changes in your CRM (e.g., send an SMS when a lead's status changes to "Qualified").

## Supported CRMs

Autoch.at currently supports direct integrations with several major CRM platforms.

- **Salesforce**
- **HubSpot**
- **Pipedrive**
- **Zoho CRM**

If your CRM is not listed, you may be able to connect it using our API or a third-party automation tool like Zapier or Make.

## Setting Up a Connection

To connect your CRM to Autoch.at:

1. Navigate to **CRM > CRM Connections**.
2. Locate your CRM provider in the list of available integrations.
3. Click **Connect**.
4. You will be redirected to your CRM provider's login page to authorize the connection.
5. Once authorized, you will be returned to Autoch.at to configure the integration settings.

## Configuring the Integration

After connecting your CRM, you need to define how data should flow between the two systems.

### Field Mapping

Field mapping tells Autoch.at which fields in its database correspond to which fields in your CRM.

For example, you might map:
- Autoch.at `Email` -> CRM `Email Address`
- Autoch.at `Phone` -> CRM `Mobile Phone`
- Autoch.at `Company` -> CRM `Account Name`

You can also map custom fields you've created in Autoch.at to custom fields in your CRM.

### Sync Settings

You can control the direction and frequency of the data sync:

- **Two-Way Sync**: Changes made in Autoch.at update the CRM, and changes made in the CRM update Autoch.at. (Recommended)
- **One-Way Sync (Autoch.at to CRM)**: Autoch.at pushes data to the CRM, but does not pull updates back.
- **One-Way Sync (CRM to Autoch.at)**: Autoch.at pulls data from the CRM, but does not push updates back.

### Activity Logging

You can choose which types of Autoch.at activities should be logged in your CRM:

- **All Conversations**: Log every interaction, regardless of channel or outcome.
- **Only Handoffs**: Only log conversations that were escalated to a human agent.
- **Only Qualified Leads**: Only log conversations where the AI assistant successfully qualified the user based on a playbook.

## Managing Connections

You can manage your active CRM connections from the **CRM Connections** page.

- **Edit Settings**: Update your field mappings or sync preferences.
- **Pause Sync**: Temporarily stop data from flowing between the systems.
- **Disconnect**: Permanently remove the integration. (This will not delete any data that has already been synced).
