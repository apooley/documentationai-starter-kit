## What is the Clients Page?

The **Clients** page (located at **CRM > Clients**) is your central hub for managing all the individuals and organizations that interact with your Autoch.at assistants and human agents.

It acts as a lightweight, built-in CRM (Customer Relationship Management) system, allowing you to view contact details, conversation history, and custom data for every person who has reached out via chat, SMS, email, voice, or social media.

## Navigating the Clients List

When you open the Clients page, you'll see a list of all your contacts.

### The Client Table

The main table displays key information for each client:
- **Name**: The client's full name (if known).
- **Email**: Their primary email address.
- **Phone**: Their primary phone number.
- **Company**: The organization they belong to.
- **Tags**: Any labels applied to their profile (e.g., "VIP", "Lead", "Support").
- **Last Active**: The date and time of their most recent interaction.

### Searching and Filtering

You can quickly find specific clients using the tools at the top of the page:
- **Search Bar**: Search by name, email, phone number, or company name.
- **Tag Filter**: Click a tag (e.g., "All", "VIP") to view only clients with that specific label.

## Managing Client Records

Autoch.at provides several tools for keeping your client database organized and up-to-date.

### Creating a Client Manually

While Autoch.at automatically creates a client record when someone new sends a message, you can also add clients manually:
1. Click the **Create Client** button.
2. Fill in their details (Name, Email, Phone, Company).
3. Add any relevant tags.
4. Click **Save**.

### Importing and Exporting

If you have an existing list of contacts, you can bring them into Autoch.at:
- **Import**: Click the **Import** button to upload a CSV file containing your client data. Autoch.at will guide you through mapping the columns in your file to the correct fields in the system.
- **Export All**: Click the **Export all** button to download your entire client list as a CSV file for use in other tools or for backup purposes.

### Reviewing Duplicates

Over time, a single person might contact you from different email addresses or phone numbers, resulting in duplicate records.

1. Click the **Review duplicates** button.
2. Autoch.at will scan your database and present potential matches based on similar names, emails, or phone numbers.
3. You can review these suggestions and choose to **Merge** the records, combining their conversation histories and contact details into a single, unified profile.

## The Client Detail View

Clicking on a client's name in the list opens their full profile.

This view provides a comprehensive look at the individual:
- **Contact Information**: Edit their name, email, phone, and company.
- **Custom Fields**: View and update any custom data points you've defined for your business (e.g., "Account ID", "Subscription Tier").
- **Conversation History**: A complete log of every interaction this client has had with your AI assistants and human agents across all channels.
- **Notes**: Add internal notes about the client that are visible only to your team.

By keeping your client records accurate and utilizing tags, you empower both your human agents and your AI assistants to provide more personalized and effective support.
