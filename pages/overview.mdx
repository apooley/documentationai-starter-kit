## The big picture

Autoch.at organizes everything into a consistent layout with a sidebar on the left, a shared header at the top, and a main content area for each page. Once you know the layout, you can move between sections quickly and find what you need without guessing.

These docs are organized to match that sidebar exactly. Each section in the navigation here corresponds to a section in the app.

<Callout kind="info">
What you see in the sidebar depends on your role and plan. If a section described in these docs doesn't appear for you, your account may not have access to it yet — reach out to your organization administrator to check your permissions.
</Callout>

## Workspaces and access

When you log in to Autoch.at, you're working inside a **workspace**. A workspace is your team's environment — it contains your AI assistants, conversations, clients, and settings.

Some organizations use multiple workspaces to separate different brands, regions, or teams. If that's the case for you, you'll be able to switch between them from the header.

Your **role** within a workspace controls what you can see and do:

- **Agents** focus on day-to-day work in the Conversations inbox.
- **Managers** can also view reports and manage client records.
- **Admins** have access to AI configuration, settings, and most workspace options.
- **Owners** have full access, including billing and user management.

If you're not sure what role you have, your organization administrator can check and update it for you.

### Signing in and session management

All pages in the main sidebar require you to be signed in. If your session expires, the app will redirect you to the login screen — just sign in again and you'll be taken back to where you were.

New users who were invited by email may need to verify their address before they can access the workspace.

## The sidebar sections

The left sidebar is your primary navigation. Here's what each section contains:

| Section | What you'll find there |
|---|---|
| **Dashboard** | A live snapshot of conversations, leads, channel status, and system health |
| **AI Assistant** | Assistants, playbooks, training context, routing rules, safety settings, and experiments |
| **Conversations** | The unified inbox for chat, SMS, email, voice, and social — plus workflows and outbound tools |
| **CRM** | Client records, CRM integrations, and lead qualification settings |
| **Insights** | Analytics, conversation intelligence, and voice performance metrics |
| **Social** | Social media publishing, queues, analytics, and the AI caption generator |
| **Settings** | Channels, branding, users, billing, and workspace preferences |

## The header

The top header bar gives you access to a few useful tools that are available across all pages:

- **Theme toggle** — Switch between light and dark mode without leaving your current screen.
- **Global search** — Jump to a conversation, client, or page from anywhere in the app.
- **Onboarding progress** — If your workspace is still being set up, you may see a progress indicator here.
- **Live activity badges** — Counters for things like unread conversations or pending items update in real time.

## How to use these docs alongside the app

The most effective way to use this documentation is to keep it open alongside the app. When you're on a page in Autoch.at, find the matching doc here and skim the headings to understand what each part of the page does.

Each doc explains:

- What the page is for and when you'd use it.
- How to complete the most common tasks on that page.
- How actions on that page affect other parts of the app.
- Which role or permission level is needed for specific actions.

## Suggested reading path

If you're new to Autoch.at, here's a logical order to work through the docs:

<Steps>

<Step title="Start with the Dashboard" icon="layout-dashboard">

The [Dashboard](/pages/overview/dashboard) gives you a feel for the app's layout and shows you what's happening in your workspace at a glance. It's a good reference point before diving into configuration.

</Step>

<Step title="Learn the inbox" icon="message-circle">

Read through [Conversations](/pages/conversations/conversations) and [Conversation Detail](/pages/conversations/conversation-detail) to understand how day-to-day work happens. Then check [Clients](/pages/crm/clients) to see how customer records connect to conversations.

</Step>

<Step title="Configure your AI" icon="zap">

Once you understand the inbox, work through [Assistants](/pages/ai-config/assistants), [Training Context](/pages/ai-config/training-context), and [Playbooks](/pages/ai-config/playbooks). These three pages are the core of how Autoch.at's AI behaves.

</Step>

<Step title="Set up your workspace" icon="settings">

Finish with [Settings](/pages/settings/users) and [Billing](/pages/settings/billing) to configure your team, channels, and plan. These pages are typically handled by an admin or owner.

</Step>

</Steps>
