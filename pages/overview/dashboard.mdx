The Dashboard is the first screen you see when you log in. It provides a high-level overview of your workspace's health, recent activity, and quick access to common tasks.

## Quick Actions

At the top of the dashboard, you'll find quick action buttons to jump straight into common workflows:
- **Test Assistant**: Opens the Widget Playground to test your AI assistant.
- **Add Knowledge**: Takes you to the Training Context page to upload documents or add website URLs.
- **View Conversations**: Opens the Conversations Inbox to review recent chats.

## Setup Wizard

If you are new to Autoch.at, you will see a **Setup Wizard** card on your dashboard. This wizard guides you through the essential steps to get your workspace ready:
1. Explore your dashboard
2. Create your first assistant
3. Add business knowledge
4. Test your setup
5. Set up widget tracking (if enabled)

You can click **Resume Setup** to continue where you left off. Once completed, the card will disappear, but you can always reopen the wizard from **Settings > Preferences**.

## System Status

The System Status panel shows the health and configuration state of your communication channels:
- **Chat Widget**: Shows if your web widget is active and configured.
- **Email**: Shows if your inbound email forwarding is set up.
- **SMS**: Shows if you have provisioned a phone number for SMS.
- **Voice**: Shows if you have provisioned a phone number for voice calls.

If a service is not yet configured, you will see a red **Setup** badge. Clicking the service will take you directly to the relevant settings page.

## Performance Metrics

The dashboard displays key metrics for the current billing period:
- **Total Conversations**: The number of unique conversations handled by your assistant.
- **Messages Sent**: The total number of messages sent by the AI.
- **Human Handoffs**: The number of times a conversation was escalated to a human agent.
- **Avg. Resolution Time**: The average time it takes to close a conversation.

## Recent Activity

The Recent Activity feed shows a real-time log of events in your workspace, such as:
- New conversations started
- Leads qualified
- Human handoffs requested
- Training context updates completed (e.g., website crawls finished)

Clicking on an activity item will take you directly to the relevant conversation or settings page.
