## Managing Your Subscription

The **Usage & Billing** page (located at **Settings > Usage & Billing**) is where workspace Owners can manage their Autoch.at subscription, view current usage, and update payment methods.

<Callout kind="info">
Only users with the **Owner** role can access the Billing page. Admins, Managers, and Agents will not see this option in their settings menu.
</Callout>

## Understanding Your Plan

Autoch.at offers different subscription tiers based on the features you need and the volume of conversations you handle.

At the top of the Billing page, you will see your **Current Plan** details:
- **Plan Name**: (e.g., Starter, Pro, Enterprise)
- **Billing Cycle**: (Monthly or Annual)
- **Next Billing Date**: When your card will be charged next.
- **Base Price**: The fixed cost of your subscription.

### Upgrading or Downgrading

If you need access to more advanced features (like custom shortlinks, advanced AI models, or more workspaces), you can upgrade your plan at any time.

1. Click the **Change Plan** button.
2. Review the available tiers and select the one that best fits your needs.
3. Confirm the change. Upgrades are typically prorated and applied immediately.

Downgrades will take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

## Tracking Usage

Autoch.at plans often include a set amount of usage (e.g., a certain number of AI-handled conversations or SMS segments per month). If you exceed these limits, you may incur overage charges.

The **Usage** section provides a real-time view of your consumption for the current billing period.

### Key Usage Metrics

- **AI Conversations**: The number of unique conversations where an AI assistant sent at least one message.
- **SMS Segments**: The number of text message segments sent or received. (Note: Long SMS messages are broken into multiple segments by carriers).
- **Voice Minutes**: The total duration of phone calls handled by Autoch.at.
- **Active Phone Numbers**: The number of phone numbers provisioned for your workspace.

By monitoring these metrics, you can anticipate your monthly costs and decide if upgrading to a higher tier with larger included allowances would be more cost-effective.

## Payment Methods and Invoices

### Updating Your Payment Method

To ensure uninterrupted service, keep your payment information up to date.

1. Scroll down to the **Payment Method** section.
2. Click **Update Payment Method**.
3. Enter your new credit card details securely via our payment processor (Stripe).
4. Save the changes.

### Viewing Invoices

You can download past invoices for your accounting records.

1. Scroll to the **Billing History** section.
2. You will see a list of all past charges, including the date, amount, and status (Paid, Pending, Failed).
3. Click the **Download** icon next to any invoice to save it as a PDF.

If a payment fails, Autoch.at will automatically retry the charge over the next few days and send an email notification to the workspace Owner. If the payment cannot be processed, your workspace may be temporarily suspended until the balance is settled.
