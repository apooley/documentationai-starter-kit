## What are Preferences?

The **Preferences** page (located at **Settings > Preferences**) contains workspace-level configuration options that affect the general behavior of your Autoch.at environment.

Unlike channel-specific settings (like Email or Chat Widget), Preferences apply broadly across all channels and features in your workspace.

## Business Hours

Defining your **Business Hours** is one of the most important configurations in Preferences. Many features in Autoch.at behave differently depending on whether a conversation occurs during or outside of your defined business hours.

For example:
- **Routing Rules** can route conversations differently after hours (e.g., "During business hours, escalate to a human. After hours, take a message and promise a callback.").
- **Handoff Messages** can be customized for after-hours scenarios (e.g., "Our team is currently offline. We'll respond first thing in the morning.").
- **Email Workflows** can trigger different sequences based on when an email arrives.

### Configuring Business Hours

1. Navigate to **Settings > Preferences**.
2. In the **Business Hours** section, select your **Timezone**.
3. For each day of the week, toggle it on or off to indicate whether it is a business day.
4. For each active day, set the **Start Time** and **End Time** for your business hours.
5. Click **Save**.

<Callout kind="tip">
**Holiday Hours**: If you need to define specific holiday closures, you can add them as exceptions in the Business Hours section. This overrides your regular schedule for specific dates.
</Callout>

## Default Language

Set the default language for your workspace. This affects the language used in system-generated messages (e.g., the initial greeting in the chat widget, automated email responses).

If your AI assistants are configured to handle multiple languages, the **Default Language** setting determines the fallback language when the customer's language cannot be detected.

## Conversation Assignment

Configure how new conversations are automatically assigned to human agents when they arrive in the inbox.

- **Round Robin**: Distribute new conversations evenly across all available agents.
- **Least Busy**: Assign the new conversation to the agent with the fewest currently open conversations.
- **Manual**: Do not automatically assign conversations. They will sit in the unassigned queue until an agent picks them up.

## Notification Defaults

Set the default notification settings for new users in your workspace. Individual users can override these defaults in their personal [Profile](/pages/settings/profile) settings.

- **New Conversation Notifications**: Should new agents be notified by email, browser push, or in-app when a new conversation is assigned to them?
- **Mention Notifications**: Should agents be notified when they are `@mentioned` in an internal note?

## Data and Privacy Defaults

Configure the default data handling behavior for your workspace.

- **Default Retention Period**: Set the default data retention policy for new workspaces (if your organization uses multiple workspaces). Individual workspace settings can override this.
- **Conversation Transcripts**: Choose whether to automatically generate transcripts for voice calls.
