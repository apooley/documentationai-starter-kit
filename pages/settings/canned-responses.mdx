## What are Canned Responses?

**Canned Responses** (located at **Settings > Canned Responses**) are pre-written message templates that your human agents can quickly insert into a conversation with a single click or keyboard shortcut.

They are designed to speed up response times for common, repetitive questions, ensuring that agents don't have to type the same answer dozens of times per day.

## Why Use Canned Responses?

Canned Responses provide several key benefits for your support and sales teams:

- **Speed**: Agents can respond to common questions in seconds instead of minutes.
- **Consistency**: Every customer gets the same accurate, approved answer to a standard question.
- **Reduced Errors**: Pre-written and reviewed responses eliminate typos and factual mistakes.
- **Onboarding**: New agents can be productive immediately, even before they've memorized all the answers.

## Creating a Canned Response

To create a new canned response:

1. Navigate to **Settings > Canned Responses**.
2. Click **Create Canned Response**.
3. Fill in the following fields:
   - **Name/Shortcut**: A short, memorable keyword that agents can type to trigger the response (e.g., `refund-policy`, `hours`, `billing-contact`). This is used for quick search within the composer.
   - **Title**: A descriptive title that helps agents identify the response in the list (e.g., "Refund Policy Explanation", "Business Hours", "Billing Contact Information").
   - **Content**: The full text of the response. You can use rich text formatting (bold, italics, lists) if the channel supports it.
4. Click **Save**.

## Using Canned Responses in the Inbox

When a human agent is composing a reply in the Conversations Inbox, they can access canned responses in two ways:

1. **Keyboard Shortcut**: Type `/` (forward slash) followed by the shortcut keyword (e.g., `/refund-policy`). A dropdown will appear with matching responses. Press Enter to insert the selected response.
2. **Toolbar Button**: Click the **Canned Responses** icon (lightning bolt) in the composer toolbar to browse the full list and search by title or keyword.

Once inserted, the agent can review and edit the canned response before sending it, allowing them to personalize it for the specific customer's situation.

## Using Personalization Variables

You can include dynamic variables in your canned responses that are automatically replaced with customer-specific information when inserted.

For example:
- `{{client.first_name}}` will be replaced with the customer's first name.
- `{{agent.name}}` will be replaced with the agent's name.

This allows you to create personalized responses at scale (e.g., "Hi {{client.first_name}}, thanks for reaching out! My name is {{agent.name}} and I'm happy to help.").

## Managing Canned Responses

As your business evolves, keep your canned responses up to date.

- **Edit**: Modify the content of a response when policies or information changes.
- **Delete**: Remove responses that are no longer relevant.
- **Search**: Use the search bar on the Canned Responses page to quickly find a specific response by its title or shortcut keyword.
