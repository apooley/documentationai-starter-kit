## Let your AI book meetings

When you connect a calendar to Autoch.at, your AI assistant can check your real-time availability and offer booking slots to customers — all within a conversation, without any back-and-forth.

Go to **Settings > Calendar** to connect your calendar.

## Supported calendar providers

- **Google Calendar** — Connect via Google OAuth
- **Microsoft Outlook / Microsoft 365** — Connect via Microsoft OAuth
- **Calendly** — Connect via Calendly OAuth

## Connecting your calendar

1. Go to **Settings > Calendar**.
2. Click **Connect Calendar**.
3. Select your calendar provider.
4. You'll be redirected to your provider's authorization page — log in and grant Autoch.at permission to read your calendar and create events.
5. Once authorized, you'll be returned to Autoch.at and your calendar will appear as connected.

<Callout kind="info">
Autoch.at only reads your calendar to check availability and creates new events when a booking is confirmed. It does not modify or delete existing events.
</Callout>

## Configuring booking settings

Once your calendar is connected, you can configure how bookings work:

- **Meeting duration** — The default length of meetings the AI will offer (e.g., 30 minutes, 1 hour).
- **Buffer time** — Padding before and after meetings so you're never booked back-to-back.
- **Availability window** — How far in advance customers can book (e.g., up to 4 weeks out).
- **Business hours** — The days and times the AI should offer as available.

## Using calendar booking in conversations

Once configured, your AI assistant can use the calendar tool in any conversation. When a customer asks to schedule a meeting, the AI will:

1. Check your real-time availability.
2. Offer a few available time slots.
3. Confirm the customer's preferred time.
4. Create the calendar event and send a confirmation.

The customer receives a calendar invite, and the event appears in your calendar automatically.

## Disconnecting a calendar

1. Go to **Settings > Calendar**.
2. Click **Disconnect** next to the calendar you want to remove.

Disconnecting a calendar stops the AI from being able to check availability or book meetings. Existing calendar events created by Autoch.at are not affected.
