## Managing your team

The **Users** page (at **Settings > Users**) is where you control who has access to your Autoch.at workspace and what they can do once they're in.

Every person who needs to use Autoch.at — whether they're an agent handling conversations, a manager reviewing reports, or an admin configuring the AI — needs to be invited here first.

## Roles and permissions

Autoch.at uses a role-based access system. When you invite someone, you assign them one of four roles:

| Role | What they can do |
|---|---|
| **Agent** | Handle conversations in the inbox, reply to customers, view their own assigned conversations |
| **Manager** | Everything an Agent can do, plus view reports, manage client records, and see all conversations |
| **Admin** | Everything a Manager can do, plus configure AI assistants, playbooks, training context, settings, and channels |
| **Owner** | Full access to everything, including billing and the ability to add or remove other Owners |

Choose the most restrictive role that still lets the person do their job. You can always upgrade a role later.

## Inviting a team member

1. Go to **Settings > Users**.
2. Click **Invite User**.
3. Enter their email address.
4. Select their role.
5. Click **Send Invitation**.

They'll receive an email with a link to set up their account. The invitation expires after 7 days — if they don't accept in time, you can resend it from the Users page.

## Changing a user's role

1. Find the user in the list.
2. Click the role dropdown next to their name.
3. Select the new role.
4. The change takes effect immediately.

## Removing a user

1. Find the user in the list.
2. Click the **...** menu next to their name.
3. Select **Remove from workspace**.

Removing a user revokes their access immediately. Their conversation history and any notes they left remain in the system — only their ability to log in is removed.

<Callout kind="warning">
You cannot remove the last Owner from a workspace. Make sure there's always at least one Owner who can manage billing and settings.
</Callout>

## Pending invitations

The Users page shows a separate list of invitations that have been sent but not yet accepted. From here you can:

- **Resend** — Send the invitation email again if the original expired or was missed.
- **Cancel** — Revoke a pending invitation before it's accepted.
