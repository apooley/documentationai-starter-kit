## What is the Conversation Detail View?

When you select a conversation from the **Conversations Inbox**, the main panel opens the **Conversation Detail** view. This is where you can read the full history of an interaction, understand the context, and take action.

The detail view is divided into three main sections:
1. **The Header**: Key information about the conversation and the customer.
2. **The Timeline**: The chronological history of messages and events.
3. **The Composer**: The area where you draft and send replies or internal notes.

## The Header

The header provides immediate context about who you are talking to and the current state of the conversation.

- **Customer Information**: The name, email address, or phone number of the person you are communicating with.
- **Channel**: An icon indicating whether the conversation is happening via Chat, SMS, Email, Voice, or Social Media.
- **Status**: Is the conversation Open, Closed, or Snoozed?
- **Assignee**: Who is currently responsible for the conversation (an AI assistant, a specific human agent, or a team).
- **Tags**: Any labels applied to the conversation (e.g., "Billing Issue", "High Priority", "VIP Customer").

### Actions in the Header

From the header, you can quickly:
- **Change Status**: Mark the conversation as resolved or reopen a closed one.
- **Reassign**: Transfer the conversation to another agent or team.
- **Manage Tags**: Add or remove tags to categorize the interaction.
- **View CRM Profile**: If the customer is linked to a CRM record, click their name to view their full profile, including past conversations and custom fields.

## The Timeline

The timeline is the core of the detail view. It displays every message and system event in the order they occurred.

### Message Types

You will see different types of entries in the timeline:

- **Customer Messages**: Messages sent by the customer, usually aligned to the left.
- **AI Responses**: Replies generated by your AI assistants, clearly marked with an AI icon.
- **Human Agent Replies**: Messages sent by your team members, usually aligned to the right.
- **Internal Notes**: Private comments left by agents, visible only to your team (not the customer). These are typically highlighted with a different background color.
- **System Events**: Automated actions, such as "Conversation assigned to Support Team" or "Handoff triggered by Routing Rule."

### AI Insights and Suggestions

Autoch.at's AI doesn't just talk to customers; it also helps your human agents.

- **AI Summaries**: For long conversations, the AI can generate a brief summary of the issue and the steps taken so far, saving you from reading the entire thread.
- **Suggested Replies**: When it's your turn to respond, the AI may offer suggested replies based on the context of the conversation and your training data. You can insert these suggestions into the composer, edit them, and send them with a single click.
- **Sentiment Analysis**: The AI may flag the customer's sentiment (e.g., "Frustrated", "Satisfied") to help you gauge the tone of the interaction.

## The Composer

The composer at the bottom of the screen is where you craft your responses.

### Replying to the Customer

By default, the composer is set to send a public reply to the customer on the channel they are using.

- **Formatting**: Use the formatting toolbar to add bold text, italics, lists, or links (depending on the channel's capabilities).
- **Attachments**: Click the paperclip icon to attach files, images, or documents.
- **Canned Responses**: Click the lightning bolt icon (or type a shortcut) to insert pre-written replies for common questions.

### Leaving Internal Notes

If you need to collaborate with a teammate or leave a reminder for yourself, switch the composer to **Internal Note** mode.

- Internal notes are added to the timeline but are completely invisible to the customer.
- You can `@mention` other agents in an internal note to notify them and draw their attention to the conversation.

### Sending and State Management

When you send a reply, you often want to change the state of the conversation at the same time. Autoch.at provides quick actions like:

- **Send and Close**: Send your reply and immediately mark the conversation as resolved.
- **Send and Snooze**: Send your reply and hide the conversation until a specific time (e.g., "Snooze until tomorrow morning").
- **Send and Keep Open**: Send your reply and leave the conversation in your active queue.
