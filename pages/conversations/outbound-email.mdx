## What is Outbound Email?

While [Email Workflows](/pages/conversations/email-workflows) automate triggered email sequences, **Outbound Email** (located at **Conversations > Outbound Email**) is designed for direct, intentional outreach.

It allows you to compose and send emails to individual clients or groups of clients from within Autoch.at, without needing to switch to a separate email client.

## Use Cases

Outbound Email is ideal for scenarios where you want to proactively reach out to a customer or a segment of your client base.

Common use cases include:
- **Proactive Support**: Notifying a specific customer about a bug fix that affected their account.
- **Sales Outreach**: Sending a personalized pitch to a list of qualified leads.
- **Announcements**: Informing a group of clients about a new feature or an upcoming maintenance window.
- **Follow-ups**: Reaching out to a client who hasn't responded to an AI-initiated conversation.

## Composing an Outbound Email

To send an outbound email:

1. Navigate to **Conversations > Outbound Email**.
2. Click **Compose New Email**.
3. In the **To** field, search for and select the recipient(s) from your [Clients](/pages/crm/clients) database, or enter an email address directly.
4. Select the **From** address (your connected sending domain, configured in [Settings > Email](/pages/settings/email)).
5. Enter a **Subject Line**.
6. Compose your message in the body editor. You can use rich text formatting, insert images, and add attachments.
7. Click **Send** to send immediately, or **Schedule** to send at a specific date and time.

## Using Email Templates

To save time and ensure consistency, you can use pre-written email templates when composing outbound messages.

1. When composing an email, click the **Templates** button.
2. Select a template from the list.
3. The template content will be inserted into the email body. You can then personalize it before sending.

To create new templates, navigate to **Settings > Email** and look for the **Email Templates** section.

## Personalization Variables

When sending to multiple recipients, you can use personalization variables to automatically insert client-specific information into your emails.

For example:
- `{{client.first_name}}` will be replaced with the recipient's first name.
- `{{client.company}}` will be replaced with their company name.
- `{{client.last_conversation_date}}` will be replaced with the date of their last interaction.

This allows you to send personalized emails at scale without manually editing each one.

## Tracking and Results

After sending an outbound email, you can track its performance:
- **Delivered**: The email was successfully delivered to the recipient's server.
- **Opened**: The recipient opened the email.
- **Clicked**: The recipient clicked a link within the email.
- **Replied**: The recipient sent a reply, which will appear in your [Conversations Inbox](/pages/conversations/conversations).
- **Bounced**: The email could not be delivered (e.g., invalid address).

Tracking data helps you understand the effectiveness of your outreach and identify which messages resonate with your audience.
