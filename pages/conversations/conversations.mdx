## Conversations overview

The Conversations page at `/conversations` is your unified inbox for all customer conversations across chat, voice, email, and other connected channels.

Each row represents a single conversation, with key context like channel, contact, preview, tags, assignee, AI sentiment and urgency, lifecycle status, and qualification so you can prioritize work at a glance.

<Callout kind="info">

Conversations are always isolated per tenant. You only see conversations that belong to your tenant workspace and that your role allows you to access.

</Callout>

## Layout and key areas

The Conversations page is organized into four main areas:

- Global search bar
- Advanced filters
- Conversations table
- Bulk actions and pagination

### Search bar

Use the search bar at the top of the page to quickly find conversations by keywords.

Typical uses include:

- Searching by **contact name** or email address
- Searching by **message content** (for example, product names, error codes)
- Narrowing down **recent interactions** with a specific topic

As you type and pause briefly, the results update automatically so you can refine your query without reloading the page.

### Advanced filters

Advanced filters let you narrow the list to the conversations that matter for your current task.

Available filters typically include:

- **Channel** – Restrict to a specific channel such as chat, voice, or email.
- **Lifecycle status** – Focus on conversations in a particular state (for example, new, active, closed).
- **Date range** – Show conversations that occurred within a specific time window.
- **Qualified** – Filter based on whether the conversation is qualified by your internal logic.
- **Workspace** – Limit results to a particular workspace within your tenant.
- **Tags** – Include only conversations that match one or more tags.
- **Assignee / My conversations** – See conversations assigned to a specific teammate or to you.

<Callout kind="info">

Filters combine with each other and with the search bar. If you see fewer results than expected, check both your search query and all active filters.

</Callout>

#### Recommended triage views

Use a few repeatable filter combinations to stay on top of your queue:

- **New and unassigned**
  - Status: new or open
  - Assignee: unassigned or not set
  - Channel: all
  - Use this view for daily triage to make sure every new conversation has an owner.

- **High urgency conversations**
  - Urgency: high (from the AI context column)
  - Status: new or active
  - Assignee: all
  - Use this view when you need to focus on time-sensitive or at-risk conversations.

- **By channel or team**
  - Channel: chat, voice, or email, depending on the team
  - Assignee: a specific teammate or group
  - Date range: today or this week
  - Use this view for channel-specific queues or to review a single teammate's workload.

Save or reuse these patterns to create a consistent triage routine for your team.

### Conversations table

The main table shows conversations in a paginated or infinite-scrolling list, with each row summarizing the most important details.

Common columns include:

- **Selection checkbox** – Select one or more rows for bulk actions.
- **Channel** – The source channel for the conversation.
- **Contact and preview** – The primary contact, a short message preview, unread indicator, and any applied tags.
- **Assignee** – The teammate currently responsible for the conversation (if any).
- **AI context** – Sentiment and urgency scores to help prioritize.
- **Status** – The current lifecycle status of the conversation.
- **Qualification** – Whether the conversation meets your qualification criteria.
- **Row actions** – Quick actions such as view, assign, update status, or delete.

Select any row or use the view action to open the full conversation detail, usually on a dedicated conversation detail page.

### Bulk actions toolbar

When you select one or more conversations, the bulk actions toolbar becomes active. From here you can:

- **Assign** conversations to a teammate.
- **Update status** for all selected conversations in one step.
- **Export selected** conversations for analysis or backup.
- **Delete selected** conversations if you no longer need them.

Bulk actions apply **only** to the conversations you have selected with the checkboxes, not to all conversations that match your filters. If you want to affect a larger set, adjust your filters, select all visible rows in that view, and then trigger the bulk action.

### Pagination and infinite loading

The list uses paginated or infinite loading behavior with a **Load more** control at the bottom.

- When more conversations exist than fit on the first page, you see a **Load more** button.
- Clicking **Load more** fetches the next batch and appends it to the list.
- Your current search and filter settings stay applied across pages.

If you scroll to the bottom and do not see **Load more**, either all results have been loaded or no results match your current filters.

## Common workflows

Use these workflows to handle the most frequent tasks on the Conversations page.

<Steps>

<Step title="Find a specific conversation" icon="search">

- Go to `/conversations` from your main navigation.
- In the **search bar**, type a keyword such as a contact name, email, or phrase from the conversation.
- Adjust **filters** (channel, status, date range, tags, assignee, workspace) to narrow down results if needed.
- Locate the matching row in the table and select it or click the view action to open the conversation.

When the search and filters match, the conversation appears in the list within a few seconds of you stopping typing.

</Step>

<Step title="Bulk assign conversations to a teammate" icon="users">

- Use search and filters to focus on the conversations you want to reassign.
- In the table, use the **checkbox** column to select the relevant conversations.
- In the **bulk actions toolbar**, choose **Assign**.
- Pick the target **assignee** from the list.
- Confirm the assignment.

The assignee column for all selected conversations updates to show the new owner. If you miss a conversation, it will not be affected, even if it matches the same filters.

</Step>

<Step title="Change lifecycle status for multiple conversations" icon="settings">

- Filter by **lifecycle status**, **assignee**, or **date range** to find the group you want to update.
- Select conversations using the **checkboxes** in the leftmost column.
- Click **Update status** in the bulk actions toolbar.
- Choose the new **lifecycle status** you want to apply.
- Confirm the change.

After the update, the **status** column for those rows reflects the new lifecycle state, while unselected conversations keep their previous status.

</Step>

<Step title="Export a set of conversations" icon="download">

- Apply **search and filters** to capture the conversations you want to export (for example, last month, qualified, specific tag).
- Select all relevant conversations using the **checkboxes**.
- Click **Export selected** in the bulk actions toolbar.
- Choose the export destination or format if prompted.
- Wait for the export to complete or check your notifications or downloads area as instructed.

The export includes only the conversations you selected at the moment you started the export, and typically contains key fields such as contact details, timestamps, tags, status, and basic AI insights.

<Callout kind="info">

Exports may include sensitive information such as customer identifiers, message previews, and internal tags. Share exported files only with trusted recipients, and follow your organization's data retention and privacy policies when storing or deleting them.

</Callout>

</Step>

<Step title="Delete conversations in bulk" icon="trash">

- Narrow the list carefully using **filters** (for example, closed conversations older than a certain date).
- Select the conversations you want to delete via the **checkbox** column.
- Click **Delete selected** in the bulk actions toolbar.
- Confirm the deletion when prompted.

The deleted conversations disappear from the table and no longer appear in new searches or filters.

<Callout kind="alert">

Deletion is often irreversible or difficult to undo. Double-check that your filters and selections are correct before you confirm bulk deletion, and avoid deleting conversations that your team may need for compliance, audits, or future analysis.

</Callout>

</Step>

</Steps>

## Realtime updates

The Conversations page subscribes to realtime events so the list stays in sync without manual refresh.

When any of the following occurs:

- A **new conversation** is created
- An existing conversation is **updated** (for example, new message, status, or assignee)
- A conversation becomes **qualified**

The list updates to reflect the change if the conversation matches your current search and filter criteria.

<Callout kind="info">

Realtime updates respect your active filters. If a conversation changes in a way that no longer matches your filters (for example, status changes from active to closed), it may disappear from your current view.

</Callout>

If realtime connectivity is temporarily unavailable, you can still manually refresh the page to fetch the latest state.

## Permissions and tenant isolation

Access to the Conversations page and specific actions depends on your tenant configuration and user role.

Key rules:

- **Tenant isolation** – Conversations are scoped to the current tenant; data is never shared across tenants.
- **Workspace scoping** – If your tenant uses workspaces, the **workspace filter** limits visibility to conversations in that workspace.
- **Role-based actions** – Some actions (such as bulk delete or export) may be restricted to admins or specific roles, even if you can see the conversations.

<Callout kind="info">

If you believe you should have access to a conversation or action but do not see it, contact your tenant admin to review your role and permissions.

</Callout>

## Empty states and error states

The Conversations page distinguishes between several empty and error conditions to guide you.

### Loading state

When the page first loads or you change filters, you see a **loading skeleton** in the table area.

- Once data arrives, the skeleton is replaced by the conversation rows.
- If loading takes longer than expected, check your network connection.

### No conversations yet

If your tenant has no conversations at all, the page shows a **true empty state** with setup guidance.

Typical next steps include:

- Connecting channels (chat, voice, email, or integrations)
- Testing an initial conversation from a demo or staging environment

Success looks like seeing the first conversation row appear after you complete setup and send a test message.

### No matches for current filters

If conversations exist but none match your current search or filters, the page shows a **no matches** message.

To recover:

- Clear one or more **filters**.
- Reset the **search bar** to an empty value.
- Confirm that the **date range** is not too narrow.

Once you relax the filters, matching conversations reappear in the table.

### Error state

If the conversations view cannot load, you see an error message with options to:

- **Refresh** the page.
- Navigate back to the **dashboard** to continue working elsewhere.

If the error persists across refreshes, contact your admin or support with any visible error details.

## Troubleshooting

Use this section to quickly diagnose common issues with the Conversations page.

### I see no results, but I know we have conversations

Check whether you are in a filtered no-results state rather than a true empty state.

- Verify that the **search bar** is clear.
- Review all active **filters**, especially:
  - Date range
  - Channel
  - Lifecycle status
  - Tags
  - Assignee or My conversations
  - Workspace
- Confirm that the **no results** message is not the setup guide for a tenant with zero conversations.

If you still do not see conversations, try a full page refresh. If the issue continues, your permissions or workspace selection may be restricting visibility.

### New conversations are not appearing in real time

If realtime updates appear to be stuck:

- Confirm that you have a **stable network connection**.
- Check whether the new or updated conversations actually match your current **filters** and **search**.
- Refresh the page once to re-establish the realtime connection.

If conversations only appear after a manual refresh, report the issue to your admin or support so they can investigate realtime connectivity for your tenant.

### I cannot perform bulk actions

If assign, update status, export, or delete are disabled:

- Make sure you have **selected at least one** conversation checkbox.
- Confirm that your **role** allows the action (for example, delete and export may be admin-only).
- Check that you are not in the middle of another **pending action** on the same selection.

If the action remains unavailable, contact your tenant admin to review your permissions.
