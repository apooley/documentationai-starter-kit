## Voice in Autoch.at

Autoch.at can handle inbound and outbound phone calls using AI voice assistants. When a customer calls one of your provisioned phone numbers, the AI assistant answers, understands the caller's intent, and either resolves the issue autonomously or transfers the call to a human agent.

All phone calls—whether handled entirely by AI or escalated to a human—appear in the **Conversations Inbox** alongside your chat, SMS, and email threads.

## Prerequisites

Before you can use voice features, you need to:

1. **Provision a Phone Number**: Navigate to **Settings > Phone Numbers** to provision a local or toll-free number for your workspace.
2. **Assign an Assistant**: In your AI assistant's configuration, ensure it is assigned to the **Voice** channel.
3. **Configure Routing**: Set up [Routing Rules](/pages/ai-config/routing-handoffs) to define what happens when the AI cannot resolve the call (e.g., transfer to a human agent or send to voicemail).

## How Voice Conversations Appear in the Inbox

When a call comes in, a new conversation is created in the inbox in real-time. The conversation entry shows:

- **Channel Icon**: A phone icon to indicate it is a voice call.
- **Caller ID**: The phone number of the caller (or their name if they are a known client in your CRM).
- **Status**: Whether the call is currently active (live), on hold, or completed.
- **Duration**: The length of the call.

## During a Live Call

If the AI is handling the call, you can monitor the conversation in real-time from the inbox. You will see a live transcript of the conversation as it unfolds.

### Taking Over a Live Call

If you determine that the AI needs human assistance during an active call, you can intervene:

1. Open the active voice conversation in the inbox.
2. Click **Join Call** or **Take Over**.
3. You will be connected to the call, and the AI will announce the transfer to the caller.

### Warm vs. Cold Transfers

When the AI transfers a call to a human agent, you can configure whether it is a:
- **Cold Transfer**: The AI immediately transfers the call to the next available agent without briefing them. The customer may need to repeat their issue.
- **Warm Transfer**: The AI first connects with the human agent privately, provides a brief summary of the caller's issue, and then bridges the customer in. This provides a seamless experience for the caller.

Configure your preferred transfer type in [Routing & Handoffs](/pages/ai-config/routing-handoffs).

## After a Call

Once a call is completed, the conversation record in the inbox is updated with:

- **Full Transcript**: An AI-generated text transcript of the entire call.
- **Call Recording**: A link to the audio recording (if recording is enabled and compliant with local regulations).
- **AI Summary**: A brief summary of the call's purpose and outcome, generated by the AI.
- **Outcome Tags**: Automatic tags applied based on the call's result (e.g., "Resolved by AI", "Escalated to Human", "Voicemail Left").

## Voicemail

If a call comes in outside of business hours, or if all human agents are busy and the AI cannot resolve the issue, the caller can leave a voicemail.

Voicemails are transcribed automatically and appear in the inbox as a new conversation, allowing your team to review and follow up during business hours.

For detailed performance metrics on your voice channel, see [Voice Analytics](/pages/insights/voice-analytics).
