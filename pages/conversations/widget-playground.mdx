## What is the Widget Playground?

The **Widget Playground** (located at **Conversations > Messaging > Widget Playground**) is a live, interactive testing environment that simulates the exact experience a customer would have when using the Autoch.at chat widget on your website.

It is an essential tool for QA testing before you deploy a new assistant, update a playbook, or add new Training Context.

## Why Use the Playground?

Testing in the playground allows you to:

- **Verify Behavior**: Confirm that your AI assistant responds correctly to different types of questions and scenarios.
- **Test Playbooks**: Walk through a complete playbook flow (e.g., lead qualification) to ensure every step works as expected.
- **Check Training Context**: Ask questions that should be answered by your uploaded documents or FAQs to verify the AI is retrieving the correct information.
- **Test Routing Rules**: Trigger keywords or phrases that should activate a routing rule to confirm the handoff logic is working.
- **Preview Branding**: See how the widget looks with your configured colors, logo, and header text before it goes live on your website.

## Using the Playground

1. Navigate to **Conversations > Messaging > Widget Playground**.
2. The playground will render the chat widget exactly as it would appear on your website.
3. Select the **Assistant** you want to test from the dropdown menu (if you have multiple assistants).
4. Start typing in the chat input and interact with the assistant.

The playground session is completely isolated from your live customer conversations. Any messages you send here will not appear in your production Conversations Inbox.

## Interpreting the Results

As you interact with the assistant in the playground, pay attention to:

- **Accuracy**: Is the AI giving correct, up-to-date answers based on your Training Context?
- **Tone**: Does the assistant's persona match your brand voice?
- **Playbook Adherence**: Is the assistant following the steps in its assigned playbook, or is it going off-script?
- **Routing Triggers**: When you type a keyword that should trigger a routing rule (e.g., "I want to speak to a human"), does the handoff occur as expected?

## Resetting the Session

If you want to start a fresh test from the beginning (e.g., to test the initial greeting or a different scenario), click the **Reset Session** button. This clears the conversation history and starts a new session, exactly as if a brand new visitor had opened the widget on your website.

<Callout kind="tip">
**Share the playground link with your team.** You can share the URL of the Widget Playground with colleagues so they can test the assistant without needing to navigate through the full dashboard. This is especially useful for getting feedback from non-technical stakeholders.
</Callout>
