## Step-by-Step Setup

### 1. Create an Assistant

Your first step is to create an AI Assistant. Navigate to **AI Assistant > Assistants** in the sidebar and click **Create Assistant**.

Give your assistant a name (e.g., "Support Bot"), select the channels it should operate on (like Chat or Email), and define its core persona.

### 2. Add Training Context

An assistant is only as good as the knowledge it has access to. Go to **AI Assistant > Training Context** to add your business information.

You can:
- Upload PDF or Word documents containing your policies
- Add links to your website for the AI to crawl
- Create specific Instructions or FAQs for common questions

### 3. Create a Playbook

Playbooks tell your assistant *what* to achieve in a conversation. Navigate to **AI Assistant > AI Playbooks** and create a new playbook.

Define the goal (e.g., "Qualify the lead and collect their email address") and attach it to the assistant you created in Step 1.

### 4. Configure Routing & Handoffs

You'll want to ensure that complex issues are escalated to human agents. Go to **AI Assistant > Routing & Handoffs**.

Set up a routing rule that triggers when a user asks to speak to a human, or when the AI detects a high-priority intent like "billing issue" or "cancel subscription".

### 5. Test in the Widget Playground

Before going live, test your setup. Navigate to **Conversations > Messaging > Widget Playground**.

Here you can interact with your assistant exactly as a customer would on your website. Test different scenarios to ensure the assistant uses the training context correctly and follows the playbook instructions.

### 6. Go Live

Once you're satisfied with the assistant's performance, you can deploy it to your live channels.

- For **Chat**, go to **Settings > Chat Widget** to get the installation code for your website.
- For **Email**, go to **Settings > Email** to connect your sending domain and configure forwarding.
- For **SMS/Voice**, go to **Settings > Phone Numbers** to provision a number.

Now you're ready to start unifying your customer interactions with Autoch.at!
