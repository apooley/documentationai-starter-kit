## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info" title="Before You Begin">
Prepare these items to complete setup quickly:
- A business email address for registration
- Credentials for your email provider (e.g., Gmail, Outlook)
- A Twilio account with a phone number for SMS
- Your website domain for chat widget installation
</Callout>

## Step 1: Create and Log In to Your Account

Follow these steps to get your Autoch.at dashboard ready.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Visit `https://autoch.at` and click **Sign Up**.

    Fill in your business email, name, and password. Verify your email via the confirmation link sent to your inbox.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Return to `https://autoch.at/login` and enter your credentials.

    Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) from your account settings for security.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 3: Connect Communication Channels

Connect your channels one by one. Use the tabs below for specific instructions.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Email" icon="mail">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Add Provider">
        In the dashboard, go to **Channels > Email** and select your provider.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Enter Credentials">
        Provide your IMAP/SMTP details:
````json
{
  "host": "imap.gmail.com",
  "port": 993,
  "username": "your-email@business.com",
  "password": "YOUR_APP_PASSWORD"
}
````
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="SMS" icon="phone">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Connect Twilio">
        Navigate to **Channels > SMS** and choose Twilio.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Add Account SID">
        Enter your Twilio credentials:
````javascript
const twilioConfig = {
  accountSid: 'YOUR_ACCOUNT_SID',
  authToken: 'YOUR_AUTH_TOKEN',
  phoneNumber: '+1234567890'
};
````
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Website Chat" icon="globe">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Generate Snippet">
        Go to **Channels > Chat** and customize your widget.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Install Code">
        Copy and paste this snippet before the closing `</body>` tag on your site:
```html
<script src="https://widget.autoch.at/YOUR_WIDGET_ID.js"></script>
```
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout kind="tip">
Test each channel by sending a message to yourself. Check the unified timeline for arrival.
</Callout>

## Step 4: Handle Your First Conversation

Send or receive a test message:

1. From your connected email or phone, message your business contact.
2. Watch it appear in the dashboard timeline.
3. Use Quiet AI to draft a reply: Click **Draft Response** and review before sending.
4. Assign ownership and set next steps.
