## Connecting Your Tech Stack

Autoch.at is designed to be the central hub for your customer communications, but it doesn't operate in a vacuum. By integrating Autoch.at with the tools you already use, you can ensure data flows seamlessly across your organization, eliminating manual data entry and providing your AI assistants with the context they need to be effective.

Integrations in Autoch.at fall into three main categories: Communication Channels, Customer Relationship Management (CRM) systems, and Custom API connections.

## Communication Channels

To unify your inbox, you must first connect the channels where your customers are trying to reach you. Autoch.at supports a wide array of native channel integrations.

### Web Chat

The Autoch.at Chat Widget is the most direct way to deploy an AI assistant. It can be installed on virtually any website by embedding a small JavaScript snippet.

We provide specific installation guides for popular platforms:
- **WordPress**: Install via a custom HTML block or a header/footer plugin.
- **Shopify**: Add the snippet to your `theme.liquid` file.
- **Webflow**: Embed the code in your project's custom code settings.

### Email

You can connect your existing support email addresses (e.g., `support@yourcompany.com`) to Autoch.at. This allows your AI assistants to draft replies or handle entire email threads automatically.

- **Custom Domains**: Verify your domain's DNS records (SPF, DKIM) within Autoch.at to send emails directly from the platform.
- **Forwarding**: Set up forwarding rules in your current provider (Google Workspace, Microsoft 365) to route incoming messages to your Autoch.at inbox.

### SMS and Voice

Autoch.at allows you to provision phone numbers directly within the platform to handle text messages and AI voice calls.

- **Local Numbers**: Provision numbers in your specific area code.
- **Toll-Free Numbers**: Set up 800 numbers for national reach.
- **Bring Your Own Carrier (BYOC)**: For enterprise customers, Autoch.at can integrate with your existing SIP trunking provider (e.g., Twilio, Telnyx) to handle voice traffic.

### Social Media

Connect your brand's social media profiles to manage direct messages and comments from the unified inbox.

- **Facebook Messenger**: Handle DMs sent to your Facebook Business Page.
- **Instagram Direct**: Manage messages sent to your professional Instagram account.
- **X (Twitter) Direct Messages**: Respond to DMs sent to your X profile.

## CRM Integrations

Connecting your CRM is crucial for keeping your customer records accurate and providing your AI assistants with personalized context.

Autoch.at offers native, two-way synchronization with several major CRM platforms. When connected, Autoch.at will automatically log conversations, update contact details, and pull relevant account information to inform the AI's responses.

### Supported Native CRMs

- **Salesforce**: Sync leads, contacts, and log conversation activities directly to Salesforce records.
- **HubSpot**: Keep your HubSpot contacts up-to-date and trigger HubSpot workflows based on Autoch.at interactions.
- **Pipedrive**: Automatically create deals and log activities when an AI assistant qualifies a lead.
- **Zoho CRM**: Sync contact data and conversation history to your Zoho account.

If you use a CRM that is not natively supported, you can often connect it using our API or a third-party automation platform.

## Custom Integrations and API

For advanced use cases, Autoch.at provides tools to build custom integrations with your proprietary software or specialized third-party tools.

### Webhooks

You can configure Autoch.at to send real-time HTTP POST requests (webhooks) to your servers whenever specific events occur.

Common webhook events include:
- A new conversation is started.
- A conversation is handed off to a human agent.
- An AI assistant successfully qualifies a lead.
- A conversation is marked as resolved.

### REST API

The Autoch.at REST API allows developers to programmatically interact with the platform. You can use the API to:
- Export conversation transcripts for external analysis.
- Update a client's custom fields based on actions taken in your own app.
- Trigger outbound SMS or email messages.
- Manage your Training Context programmatically.

### Automation Platforms

If you don't have developer resources, you can use third-party automation platforms to connect Autoch.at to thousands of other apps.

- **Zapier**: Create "Zaps" to connect Autoch.at with tools like Slack, Google Sheets, or Mailchimp.
- **Make (formerly Integromat)**: Build complex, multi-step workflows integrating Autoch.at with your broader tech stack.
