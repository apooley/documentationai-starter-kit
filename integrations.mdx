## Connect your stack

Autoch.at integrates with the tools your business already relies on. Connections are managed in **Settings** and in the **CRM** section of the platform.

## Communication channels

These are the channels your AI assistant and team can use to communicate with customers:

| Channel | How to connect |
|---|---|
| **Website Chat Widget** | Install the JavaScript snippet from **Settings > Chat Widget** |
| **Email** | Configure your domain in **Settings > Email** with SPF/DKIM authentication |
| **SMS** | Provision a phone number in **Settings > Phone Numbers** |
| **Voice (Phone)** | Provision a phone number in **Settings > Phone Numbers** and configure your AI assistant for voice |
| **Facebook Messenger** | Connect your Facebook Page in **Social Media > Profiles** |
| **Instagram DMs** | Connect your Instagram Business account in **Social Media > Profiles** |
| **LinkedIn DMs** | Connect your LinkedIn Company Page in **Social Media > Profiles** |
| **X (Twitter) DMs** | Connect your X Business account in **Social Media > Profiles** |

## CRM integrations

Sync your client records and conversation data with your CRM:

| CRM | What syncs |
|---|---|
| **HubSpot** | Contacts, companies, deals, notes |
| **Salesforce** | Contacts, leads, accounts, activities |
| **Pipedrive** | Contacts, deals, notes |
| **Zoho CRM** | Contacts, leads, activities |

Connect your CRM at **CRM > CRM Connections**.

## Calendar integrations

Allow your AI assistant to check availability and book meetings:

| Calendar | Notes |
|---|---|
| **Google Calendar** | Full read/write access for availability checking and booking |
| **Microsoft Outlook / Office 365** | Full read/write access for availability checking and booking |
| **Calendly** | Connect your Calendly account to let the AI share your booking link |

Connect your calendar at **Settings > Calendar Integration**.

## AI model providers

By default, Autoch.at uses its own managed AI infrastructure. If you want to use your own API keys for cost control, compliance, or to use specific model versions, you can bring your own keys:

| Provider | Notes |
|---|---|
| **OpenAI** | Use your own GPT-4 or GPT-4o API key |
| **Anthropic** | Use your own Claude API key |

Configure BYOK at **Settings > Bring Your Own Key**.

## Webhooks and API

For integrations not listed above, Autoch.at provides:

- **Webhooks** — Send real-time event notifications to any URL when conversations are created, updated, or resolved; when leads are qualified; or when other events occur.
- **REST API** — Programmatically access and manage conversations, clients, and other data.

Webhook configuration is available in **Settings**. API documentation is available at [api.autoch.at/docs](https://api.autoch.at/docs).

## Zapier and Make

Autoch.at connects to Zapier and Make (formerly Integromat), giving you access to thousands of additional integrations without writing any code. Search for "Autoch.at" in the Zapier or Make app directories to find available triggers and actions.
