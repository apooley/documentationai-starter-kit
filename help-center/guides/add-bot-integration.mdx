## Overview

Autoch.at supports multiple channels — website chat, SMS, email, voice, and social media DMs. This guide walks you through connecting a new channel so conversations from that channel start flowing into your inbox.

## Connecting the website chat widget

The chat widget is the fastest channel to set up.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Chat Widget settings">
    Navigate to **Settings > Chat Widget**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure the widget">
    Set the widget's display name, greeting message, and color to match your brand. You can preview changes in real time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy the installation snippet">
    Click **Copy Code** to copy the JavaScript snippet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install on your website">
    Paste the snippet into your website's HTML just before the closing `</body>` tag. If you're using WordPress, use a plugin like **Insert Headers and Footers** or add it directly in your theme's footer template.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test it">
    Visit your website and start a test conversation to confirm the widget is working.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Connecting email

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Email settings">
    Navigate to **Settings > Email**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your domain">
    Enter the domain you want to receive emails on (e.g., `support@yourcompany.com`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish DNS records">
    Autoch.at will provide SPF, DKIM, and MX records to add to your domain's DNS. Add these records in your domain registrar or DNS provider.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify the connection">
    Click **Verify** after publishing the DNS records. Verification may take a few minutes while DNS propagates.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up forwarding (if needed)">
    If you already have an email address receiving messages, set up forwarding from that address to your Autoch.at inbound address.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Connecting SMS

<Steps>
  <Step title="Provision a phone number">
    Go to **Settings > Phone Numbers** and click **Get a Number**. Choose a local or toll-free number.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign it to SMS">
    Go to **Settings > SMS** and select the number you just provisioned as your SMS number.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test it">
    Send a text message to the number and confirm it appears in your Conversations inbox.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Connecting social media

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Social Profiles">
    Navigate to **Social Media > Profiles**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your account">
    Click **Connect Account**, select your platform (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, or X), and follow the authorization flow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the connection">
    Once authorized, the account will appear as connected. New DMs from that platform will now flow into your inbox.
  </Step>
</Steps>
