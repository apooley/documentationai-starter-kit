## Connect Your Bot

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Integrations">
    Go to the 'Integrations' tab in your project menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Bot Platform">
    Choose your bot platform (e.g., Slack, Discord).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill Bot Details">
    Enter Bot Token and callback URL as required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save & Test">
    Click 'Save' and then 'Test Connection' to ensure it's active.
  </Step>
</Steps>
