## How long does it take to set up Autoch.at?

Most teams have their AI assistant live and handling real conversations within 30 minutes. The [Quickstart guide](/quickstart) walks you through the whole process.

## Do I need technical skills to get started?

No. The core setup — training your AI, creating playbooks, and installing the chat widget — requires no coding. The widget installation is a simple copy-paste of a JavaScript snippet into your website's HTML.

## What's the first thing I should do after signing up?

Start with your **Training Context**. Go to **AI Assistant > Training Context** and enter your website URL. Autoch.at will crawl your site and extract your content automatically, giving your AI a solid knowledge base right away.

## How do I install the chat widget on my website?

1. Go to **Settings > Chat Widget**.
2. Copy the installation code snippet.
3. Paste it into your website's HTML, just before the closing `</body>` tag.
4. Visit your website to confirm the widget appears.

If you're using WordPress, you can paste the snippet using a plugin like **Insert Headers and Footers** or directly in your theme's footer template.

## Can I test the AI before putting it on my website?

Yes — use the **Widget Playground** at **Conversations > Messaging > Widget Playground**. It's a live sandbox where you can interact with your AI assistant exactly as a customer would, without it being on your actual website.

## How do I invite my team?

Go to **Settings > Users** and click **Invite User**. Enter their email address and select their role. They'll receive an invitation email with a link to set up their account.
