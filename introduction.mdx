## What is Autoch.at?

Autoch.at is an AI-powered customer engagement platform. It lets you deploy AI assistants that handle customer conversations across every channel — your website chat widget, SMS, email, phone, and social media DMs — all flowing into one unified inbox.

Your AI assistants can answer questions, qualify leads, book meetings, route conversations to the right team, and escalate to a human when the situation calls for it. When a human agent takes over, they have full context from everything the AI already discussed.

The result: faster responses, more consistent service, and a team that spends less time on repetitive questions and more time on the conversations that actually need a human touch.

## What you can do with Autoch.at

**Handle conversations across every channel.** Chat, SMS, email, voice, and social media DMs all land in one inbox. Your team never has to check multiple tools.

**Deploy AI assistants that actually work.** Configure your AI with your business knowledge, set up playbooks for common scenarios, and let it handle the volume — 24/7, without burning out.

**Qualify leads automatically.** Define your qualification criteria and let the AI ask the right questions, collect the right information, and flag the leads worth your team's time.

**Keep humans in the loop.** Set up routing rules and handoff triggers so the AI escalates at exactly the right moment. Your team can take over any conversation with full context.

**Understand what's happening.** Analytics, conversation intelligence, and voice analytics give you a clear picture of what customers are asking, how your AI is performing, and where to improve.

**Manage your social presence.** Publish posts, manage queues, and handle social DMs — all from the same platform.
