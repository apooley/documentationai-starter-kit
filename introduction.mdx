## Meet <a href="http://Autoch.at" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Autoch.at</a>

## Tour the dashboard

The Autoch.at dashboard is your control center for conversations, AI behavior, and workspace settings.

Use the navigation sidebar to move between:

- The live inbox and conversation list

- AI configuration for assistants, playbooks, and routing

- Settings for channels, branding, privacy, and billing

For a visual overview of each area and how they connect, see the [Dashboard](/pages/overview/dashboard) page.

<Callout kind="info" collapsed="false">
  You need an Autoch.at account and the right workspace permissions to access some pages in this guide, such as Settings and AI Config.
</Callout>

## First-time setup steps

Follow these steps in order to reach your first real conversation on the unified timeline.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your workspace and sign in" icon="user" title-type="p">
    Sign up or accept your invite, then sign in to `https://dashboard.autoch.at`. Confirm that you can see the main dashboard and navigation sidebar.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect your communication channels" icon="link" title-type="p">
    Set up your primary email domain and any SMS or chat channels you plan to use. This ensures new messages flow into Autoch.at instead of separate tools.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Explore the dashboard basics" icon="layout-dashboard" title-type="p">
    Open the [Dashboard](/pages/overview/dashboard) docs in a new tab and skim the key areas: navigation, search, and conversation detail. This helps you know where everything lives before you go live.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure your first AI assistant" icon="zap" title-type="p">
    Go to [Assistants](/pages/ai-config/assistants) to set up how AI participates in your workflows. Start with conservative settings, then expand to drafting, summarization, and suggested replies.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add routing, playbooks, and training context" icon="settings" title-type="p">
    Build out your first [Playbooks](/pages/ai-config/playbooks), connect them to [Routing & Handoffs](/pages/ai-config/routing-handoffs), and add key documents to [Training context](/pages/ai-config/training-context) so AI aligns with your policies.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Test an end-to-end conversation" icon="play-circle" title-type="p">
    Send a test email or chat, respond with AI assistance, and confirm the full interaction appears on a single timeline in [Conversations](/pages/conversations/conversations).
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="info" collapsed="false">
  For a more detailed, hands-on walkthrough including screenshots and example configurations, continue with the [Quickstart](/quickstart).
</Callout>
