## Core Platform Features

Autoch.at is built to unify your customer communications and automate repetitive tasks using advanced AI. Here is a breakdown of the key features available on the platform.

### AI Assistants & Automation

- **Multi-Assistant Architecture**: Create specialized AI agents for different roles (e.g., Support, Sales, Reception).
- **Custom Personas**: Define the exact tone, personality, and behavioral guidelines for each assistant.
- **Playbooks**: Build version-controlled, goal-oriented conversational flows (e.g., Lead Qualification, Troubleshooting).
- **Training Context**: Upload documents (PDF, DOCX), sync website URLs, and create FAQs to build a custom knowledge base.
- **Routing Rules**: Automatically transfer conversations between assistants or to human teams based on keywords, intent, or channel.
- **Handoff Guardrails**: Prevent abuse with limits on handoffs per conversation, cooldown periods, and loop prevention.
- **AI Safety System**: Enforce brand safety with blocked keywords and "Serious Mode" triggers for sensitive inquiries.
- **Playbook Experiments**: Run A/B tests on different playbook versions to optimize conversion and resolution rates.
- **Messaging Directives**: Apply global rules and formatting instructions across all assistants.

### Unified Inbox & Channels

- **Omnichannel Inbox**: Manage Chat, SMS, Email, Voice, and Social Media conversations from a single, real-time timeline.
- **Web Chat Widget**: Customizable website widget with proactive launch rules (time on page, scroll depth, exit intent).
- **SMS & Voice**: Provision phone numbers directly within Autoch.at to handle text messages and AI voice calls.
- **Email Integration**: Connect custom domains to send and receive support emails.
- **Social Media Integration**: Connect social profiles to manage direct messages and comments.
- **Email & SMS Workflows**: Automate asynchronous follow-ups, auto-responders, and status-based messaging sequences.
- **Internal Notes & Mentions**: Collaborate with teammates privately within a customer thread.
- **Canned Responses**: Quickly insert pre-written replies for common human-handled questions.
- **AI Suggested Replies**: Get AI-generated draft responses based on the conversation context when a human takes over.

### CRM & Client Management

- **Built-in Lightweight CRM**: Automatically create and manage profiles for every contact.
- **Conversation History**: View the complete, cross-channel history of every interaction with a specific client.
- **Custom Tags & Fields**: Organize clients and conversations with custom labels and data points.
- **Duplicate Merging**: Automatically detect and merge duplicate contact records.
- **Import/Export**: Easily move client data in and out of Autoch.at via CSV.
- **External CRM Connections**: Two-way sync with major CRMs (Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, Zoho) to keep your system of record up to date.

### Insights & Analytics

- **Real-time Dashboard**: Monitor live conversation volume, active channels, and system health.
- **Performance Analytics**: Track resolution times, first response times, and handoff rates.
- **Conversation Intelligence**: Analyze trends, missed follow-ups, and objections across all interactions.
- **FAQ Analytics**: Identify gaps in your Training Context by tracking FAQ hits, misses, and near-misses.
- **Scheduled Reports**: Automatically generate and email performance reports to stakeholders.
- **Voice Analytics**: Deep dive into call durations, transcripts, and AI voice performance.

### Security & Administration

- **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)**: Assign Owner, Admin, Manager, or Agent roles to control permissions.
- **PII Redaction**: Automatically detect and mask sensitive information (credit cards, SSNs) before it is stored or processed.
- **Data Retention Policies**: Configure automatic deletion of old conversations to comply with privacy regulations (GDPR, CCPA).
- **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)**: Secure user accounts with mandatory or optional 2FA.
- **Custom Shortlinks**: Use your own branded domain for shortened URLs sent via SMS.
- **Workspaces**: Manage multiple distinct brands or departments under a single organization account.
