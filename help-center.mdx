## Welcome to the Help Center

The Autoch.at Help Center is your resource for resolving issues, understanding best practices, and getting the most out of your AI assistants. Whether you're setting up your first playbook or troubleshooting a complex routing rule, you'll find the information you need here.

If you cannot find the answer to your question in these articles, please reach out to our support team via the chat widget in your Autoch.at dashboard.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Our FAQs cover the most common questions new users have when getting started with the platform.

### Getting Started

- **How do I connect my email domain?** Learn the steps to verify your domain and configure forwarding so your support emails appear in the unified inbox.
- **What is the difference between an Assistant and a Playbook?** Understand how personas (Assistants) and goals (Playbooks) work together to create effective AI agents.
- **How do I add the chat widget to my website?** Find the installation code and instructions for embedding the widget on your site.

### Billing and Accounts

- **How do I upgrade my plan?** Instructions for workspace Owners on how to change subscription tiers and manage payment methods.
- **How do I invite a new team member?** Learn how to add users and assign them the appropriate roles (Admin, Manager, Agent).

## Troubleshooting

If something isn't working as expected, our troubleshooting guides can help you identify and resolve the issue.

### AI Behavior

- **Why is my AI assistant giving the wrong answer?** Learn how to diagnose issues with your Training Context and ensure the AI is prioritizing the correct information.
- **Why didn't the conversation hand off to a human?** Troubleshoot your Routing Rules and Handoff Settings to ensure guardrails aren't blocking necessary escalations.
- **Why is the AI ignoring my Playbook instructions?** Discover common reasons why an assistant might deviate from its assigned goals and how to fix them.

### Channels and Integrations

- **Why aren't my emails showing up in the inbox?** Steps to verify your email forwarding rules and domain authentication (SPF/DKIM).
- **Why isn't the chat widget appearing on my site?** Check your installation code, allowed domains, and proactive launch rules.
- **Why isn't my CRM syncing?** Troubleshoot connection issues, field mappings, and sync settings for Salesforce, HubSpot, and other CRMs.

## Detailed Guides

For more complex setups, our detailed guides provide step-by-step instructions and best practices.

### Configuration

- **Building an Effective Training Context**: Learn how to structure your documents, FAQs, and website links for optimal AI retrieval.
- **Designing a Lead Qualification Playbook**: A comprehensive guide to creating a playbook that asks the right questions and accurately scores leads.
- **Setting up Advanced Routing Rules**: How to use intent detection, keywords, and business hours to route conversations to the right team.

### Operations

- **Managing the Unified Inbox**: Best practices for human agents on how to monitor, take over, and resolve conversations efficiently.
- **Using Conversation Intelligence**: How to analyze trends, identify missed follow-ups, and improve your AI's performance using the Analytics dashboard.
- **Configuring AI Safety and Serious Mode**: A guide to protecting your brand and ensuring sensitive inquiries are handled appropriately.
